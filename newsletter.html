A $75,000 DEADLINE:

What the Polo Club's New Capital Contribution Means for Buyers and Sellers

By Samantha Gornstein | The Edmund Bogen Team at Douglas Elliman Real Estate

As South Florida's season winds down, most conversations in the market shift toward summer slowdowns and off-season strategy. But for anyone with their eye on The Polo Club of Boca Raton—one of Palm Beach County's most storied and sought-after luxury communities—the next few weeks may represent the most consequential window of opportunity in recent memory.

Effective May 31, 2026, the Polo Club's capital contribution will increase from $250,000 to $325,000. Buyers who move quickly stand to save $75,000 at the table. The April 16th contract deadline creates a rare, hard-edged opportunity.
